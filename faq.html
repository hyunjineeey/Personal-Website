Why do you code? 코딩을 하는 이유가 있으신가요?

I believe that coding can change the world. There are many things that can be improved with coding. From small inconveniences to how an entire task is performed. Let’s suppose you have some inconveniences in your life and you want to make it better. Coding can fix it. All you need is a computer. You can make a program whenever or wherever you like, such as cafes, parks, even in your dorm. I believe through a completed program, one may receive great help in their daily lives and through this help they could live a happier life. Isn't it cool to contribute to someone's life? I'm putting a lot of energy into coding so that someone can live a better life. 코딩, 이것 하나면 세상을 변화시킬 수 있다고 믿어요. 살면서 내 마음대로 안 되는 것들이 정말 많은데, 코딩으로는 안 되는 게 없어요. 만약 본인이 생활하면서 불편한 점이 있거나 꼭 필요한 프로그램이 있다고 가정해봅시다. 필요한 건 딱 하나, 컴퓨터에요. 컴퓨터 하나만 있으면 카페, 공원, 기숙사 등 어디서든 시작할 수 있어요. 그리고 그렇게 완성된 프로그램이 어떤 누군가의 생활에 큰 도움을 주고, 그로 인해 그/그녀가 행복한 하루를 살아간다고 생각해봤어요. 어떤 누군가의 인생에 기여한다는 것, 정말 멋지지 않나요? 저는 그 누군가의 삶이 더 나은 삶이 될 수 있도록 열심히 코딩하는 중입니다.

How did you study English? 영어 공부는 어떻게 하셨나요?

I listen to a lot of Podcasts. It is a great way to fill time while commuting, walking, working out, doing household tasks, etc. I feel like I listen to Podcasts more than Music. It has lots of different topics like IT, environment, daily life or history, etc. You could improve not only your listening skills also a knowledge in the field you are interested in. Here is a list of my favorite Podcasts.
What is Currently Queued Up : Do By Friday, TED Radio Hour, Rooster Teeth Podcast
Past Favorites/Only for Studying English : All Ears English, English Conversation with Story (in Korean)

Besides, I write a diary in English. I have been writing a diary for a few years in Korean. Since I thought I needed to improve my writing skills, I started writing in English instead. I found that there are lots of ways to study English skills except for writing. You could even improve your listening and speaking skills when you have a conversation with native people. However, writing seems a little different. I didn’t write in English except for when I’m taking a composition class at school. So I decided to start and of course, I don’t do it every day but at least three times a week.

Note: I’m still learning. Please help me to improve. If you feel my English pronunciation or grammar is awkward, please don't hesitate to correct me. Thank you.

How do you overcome when you are having a hard time? 힘든 일이 생겼을 땐 어떻게 극복하시나요?

First, I like to read books. I take a break from what I’m currently doing and read books when I’m stressed. Surprisingly, reading makes me calm. Every time I read a book, if I find a sentence that touches my heart, I tend to write it down in my notebook. It has all the great sentences from the books I’ve read so far. Whenever I read this notebook, I get motivated again. It is one of my treasures.

In addition, I FaceTime with my parents. Everytime I FaceTime with them, I feel calm for no reason. It is just a simple conversation and it makes me feel happy. I think that’s why I FaceTime with them every night. They’re the ones who love and support me all the time. How can I repay them for their love?

Lastly, I look at the list of wise sayings by Ms. Kim, who was my homeroom teacher when I was a high school senior. She told us a lot of wise sayings in class. I wrote them in my notebook every time she said them because I didn’t want to forget. Whenever I read them, I get motivated and it reminds me of the happy memories from my school days. Here is an example of one of her wise sayings that I’m most impressed with.

"I think that I am raising 33 leaders." - Wednesday, August 14, 2013 AM 9:29
