The Korean government strengthened quarantine procedures for every immigrant because of the rapid spread of the COVID-19. As of March 27, 2020, people who are entering from Europe must get a test before immigration, and people who are entering from the United States must self quarantine for 14 days, regardless of symptoms.

I did not move from my seat until I arrived, wearing a mask and gloves because of the risk of infection. I did not drink water as well as eat meals on the flight.
